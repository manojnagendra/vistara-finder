Understanding the Vistara Merger

01

The Flight Number Code

Air India tags all flights operated by former Vistara aircraft with flight numbers starting with "2" (e.g., AI 2955, AI 2810). Standard Air India flights use 3-digit or standard 8xx/4xx series numbers.

02

Narrowbody Lie-Flat Beds

Ex-Vistara Airbus A321neo aircraft feature 12 fully lie-flat business class seats in a 2-2 configuration. Standard narrowbodies in India (including standard Air India) only feature standard recliners.

03

Premium Economy Cabin

Vistara was the pioneer of Premium Economy in India. Transferred aircraft offer a dedicated Premium Economy cabin with 33-38" pitch, leg rests, and premium meals, which standard Air India is only beginning to roll out.

04

Seatback Entertainment

All ex-Vistara A321neo and Boeing 787-9 aircraft have functioning personal seatback entertainment screens. In contrast, standard Air India older narrowbodies do not feature seatback entertainment screens.
